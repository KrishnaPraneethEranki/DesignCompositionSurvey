Client - PALM TREE CAFE

PALM TREE CAFE - is a rooftop themed cafe bar which sells both liquor and other refreshments like coffee, tea, snacks etc.

The task is to design a brand identity to this place

Client - THE GRILL RESTAURANT

The restaurant offers a fine style of delicate cooking and brings a delightful variety of grills from all around the world. The food offered at The Grill consists Sizzlers barbeques and Indian grilling styles. Right from the kebabs till your barbeques the Grill ensures your taste buds a culinary delight.

Crack the season’s joy with some juicy BBQ at the Grill.Scrumptious starters will be served on the table. While you enjoy the delectable foods, you will be more than surprised with the unlimited Indo – Continental cuisine, and pre-plated desserts. Come and unwind by the poolside under the vast sky.

The task is to design a print advertisement.

Client - SPOTIFY

As a part of designing SPOTIFY's possible new feature on improving user engagement, we need to understand the user journey of people who are not much of a passionate music lover but still would retain Spotify app.

The task is to design a creative explaining the user journeys

Client - Easyday

Easyday is a retail super store that has been in business for almost 40 years. Due to the Covid pandemic, Easyday R&D came up with an idea of an online store where the user can compare each item with its peers as a customer generally does in a store.

The main objective of this app is to retain the physical shopping experience without actually getting into a store.

The task is to design UI/UX for this project.
